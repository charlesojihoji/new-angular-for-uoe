The University of England (UOE) is one of the oldest universities in the world and definitely in England. It was founded by Lord Thomas Bolder in 1545. UOE is a fully accredited university and it ranks in the "Top Best 250 Colleges and Univeristies" in the world.

Although UOE is mainly a liberal arts university, it does offer a few Bachelor of Science degrees such as in Biomedical Engineering, Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering. UOE also offers Law degrees and it has a Medical School.
